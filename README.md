## 🧠 Why Patterns?
Instead of solving 1000 random questions, I am focusing on **Patterns**. 
- 🔗 **Two Pointers:** Reduces $O(n^2)$ to $O(n)$.
- 🏎️ **Fast & Slow:** Perfect for cycle detection.
- 🪟 **Sliding Window:** Best for contiguous subarrays.
